How to Use the Chatbot

  1. Type Your Question: Use the input box at the bottom of the chatbot to enter your financial question (e.g., "How do I start a Roth IRA?").
  2. Click the Send Button: Press the paper plane icon or hit Enter to submit your question.
  3. View the Response: The chatbot will reply with concise and actionable advice. Responses may include step-by-step instructions or explanations.
  4. Continue the Conversation: You can ask follow-up questions to get additional details or clarify the advice.
  5. Stay Organized: The chat history is preserved, so you can review earlier responses as needed.
